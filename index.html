Our Story

Born from a love of waffles & music

Rock n Roll Waffle was born in the heart of Vesu, Surat — a small stall with a big dream. We believed that waffles deserved the same energy as a rock concert: loud, bold, and absolutely unforgettable.

Every waffle we serve is crafted with premium ingredients, cooked golden-crispy on cast-iron irons, and topped with flavours that hit harder than a guitar riff.

From classic butter to berry explosions — something for every legend who walks up to our stall.
