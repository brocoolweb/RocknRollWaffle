Our Story

Born from a love of waffles & music

Rock N Roll Waffles was born in the heart of Vesu, Surat — a small stall with a massive dream. We believed waffles deserved the same energy as a rock concert: loud, bold, and absolutely unforgettable.

Every waffle is crafted with premium ingredients, cooked golden-crispy on cast-iron irons, and topped with flavours that hit harder than a guitar riff. From classic butter to Pistachio Kunafa — something for every legend.

We also accept party orders and waffle cakes — because every celebration deserves to rock!
